S2 Manual
We've made a manual available that covers every topic related to using S2 on LiveJournal. As a matter of fact you're currently reading a chapter from the manual. To start from the beginning, navigate to S2 Manual.
LJ Style Discussion»
LJ Style Discussion» is a community on LiveJournal.com» that covers most S1 and S2 discussions. This community has been sanctioned by administrators of LiveJournal as an ‘official’ community.
S2 Practice Discussion»
S2 Practice Discussion» is a community on LiveJournal.com» that deals with introducing S2 to members of LiveJournal's support team. The introductory text available in the community was actually used to write portions of this chapter.

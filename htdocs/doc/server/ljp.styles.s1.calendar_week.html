CALENDAR_WEEK

CALENDAR_WEEK — HTML for a week in the calendar. 4-5 of these week objects get concatenated together into the %%weeks%% property of the CALENDAR_MONTH.

View Types:

calendar

Overrideable:

No; users of the style cannot override this. It may only be defined in the style.

Properties

days The days in the week, made up from a bunch of CALENDAR_DAY variables.
emptydays_beg The empty days at the beginning of the month, from the CALENDAR_EMPTY_DAYS variable.
emptydays_end The empty days at the end of the month, from the CALENDAR_EMPTY_DAYS variable.

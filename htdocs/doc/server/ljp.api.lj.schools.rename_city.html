LJ::Schools::rename_city

LJ::Schools::rename_city — Renames a city within a country and state.

Use

LJ::Schools::rename_city(countrycode, statecode, fromcitycode, tocitycode);

Arguments

  • countrycode. The country the city to rename is in.

  • statecode. The state the city to rename is in.

  • fromcitycode. Origin citycode.

  • tocitycode. Destination citycode.

Source:

cgi-bin/schoollib.pl

Returns:

1 on success, undef on error.

CALENDAR_YEAR_DISPLAYED

CALENDAR_YEAR_DISPLAYED — Some HTML that goes in the CALENDAR_YEAR_LINKS for the displayed year.

View Types:

calendar

Overrideable:

No; users of the style cannot override this. It may only be defined in the style.

Variable Type

HTML

Properties

yyyy The 4 digit year that's being displayed.
yy The 2 digit year that's being displayed.

LJ::get_sent_invites

LJ::get_sent_invites — Get a list of sent invitations from the past 30 days.

Use

LJ::get_sent_invites(cuserid);

Arguments

  • cuserid. a userid or u object of the community to get sent invitations for

Source:

cgi-bin/communitylib.pl

Returns:

hashref of arrayrefs with keys userid, maintid, recvtime, status, args (itself a hashref of what abilities the user would be given)

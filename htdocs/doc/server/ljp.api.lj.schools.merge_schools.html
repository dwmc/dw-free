LJ::Schools::merge_schools

LJ::Schools::merge_schools — Merges schools into one record.

Use

LJ::Schools::merge_schools(parentsid, childsids);

Arguments

  • parentsid. The master/parent schoolid to merge the other schools into.

  • childsids. Arrayref of schoolids to merge into the parentsid.

Source:

cgi-bin/schoollib.pl

Returns:

1 on success, undef on error.

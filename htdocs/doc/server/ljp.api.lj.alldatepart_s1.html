LJ::alldatepart_s1

LJ::alldatepart_s1 — Gets date in MySQL format, produces s1dateformat.

Use

LJ::alldatepart_s1(void);

Info

s1 dateformat is: "%a %W %b %M %y %Y %c %m %e %d %D %p %i %l %h %k %H" Sample string: Tue Tuesday Sep September 03 2003 9 09 30 30 30th AM 22 9 09 9 09. Thu Thursday Oct October 03 2003 10 10 2 02 2nd AM 33 9 09 9 09

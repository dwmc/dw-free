LASTN_DATE_FORMAT

LASTN_DATE_FORMAT — Describes how dates/times are formatted in each LASTN_EVENT variable.

View Types:

lastn

Overrideable:

Yes; users of this style may override this

Variable Type

HTML

Properties

dayshort Day of week. Short form. Example: Mon, Tue, Wed...
daylong Day of week. Long form. Example: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday...
monshort Month. Short form. Example: Jan, Feb, Mar...
monlong Month. Long form. Example: January, February, March...
yy Year, two digit.
yyyy Year, four digit.
m Month. 1-12.
mm Month. 01-12.
d Day. 1-31.
dd Day. 01-31.
dth Day plus ordinal. 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th...
ap AM or PM as a one-character lower-case "a" or "p".
AP AM or PM as a one-character upper-case "A" or "P".
ampm AM or PM as a two-character lower-case "am" or "pm".
AMPM AM or PM as a two-character upper-case "AM" or "PM".
min Minutes. 00-59.
12h Hour. 1-12.
12hh Hour. 01-12.
24h Hour. 1-23.
24hh Hour. 01-23.

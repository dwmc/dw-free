DAY_NOEVENTS

DAY_NOEVENTS — When no events occur on a given day in the day view, this variable gets inserted into %%events%% of DAY_PAGE.

View Types:

day

Overrideable:

No; users of the style cannot override this. It may only be defined in the style.
